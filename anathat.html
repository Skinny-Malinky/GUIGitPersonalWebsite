An' A That

"An' A' That" is an app made for a specific poetry community. I address the problem that many people have with Burns' poetry in that they only ever read it in school. I want to introduce a more playful element in the way that people interact with Burns poetry and allow them to explore it without having to worry about academic pressures.

The interaction of tearing up paper is an important part of the experience as it is a cathartic act. Prototyping the "snapping into place" of each of the torn words is important and I would add further animations and sound effects to improve the prototype.
