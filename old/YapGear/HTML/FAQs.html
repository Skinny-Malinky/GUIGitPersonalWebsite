FAQs

How do I log in?

To log into your profile; Enter your username and password into the login box to the right.

How to I upload my stories?

First, you will need to choose the section of which your story comes under. The choices are The Good,The Bad and The Funny. Once you've chosen the section which best fits your story. Choose the 'upload' Icon and then follow the steps in there.

How do I get in contact with administrators?

Click here and you will be taken to the contact us page, this includes an email link to administration staff who will get back to you A.S.A.P. Alternatively, you could post on the Facebook group and a member of staff or user will help solve your issue.

How does the 'Possible Places' page work?

The Possible Places page works through a linked map image. Clicking on the area of which you are interested in will take you to a dedicated section about that area.
