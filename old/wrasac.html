WRASAC
WRASAC stands for "Women's Rape and Sexual Abuse Clinic". During my time at college studying "New Media" we were given a live project to design a website for WRASAC and they would choose their favourite design. Unfortunately, mine didn't win, but it did give me some good web design practice. We were only required to make a few pages; not all of it works.
